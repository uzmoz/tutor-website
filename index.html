Обо мне — резюме и достижения

Hi, I’m Nick Shubin, an English tutor helping teens and adults gain real speaking confidence, improve pronunciation, and master English for study, work, and everyday communication.

I teach online via Zoom, Mira, and Notion, offering structured lessons with a modern, interactive approach. I design personalised plans based on goals, level, and learning style.
